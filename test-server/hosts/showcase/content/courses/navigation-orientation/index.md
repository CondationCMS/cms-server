## Know where you are

We translate contour lines into real terrain, take bearings and plan a route of our own. Smartphones and GPS are included where they make sense — as useful tools, never as the only answer.

## What you will learn

- Read topographic maps
- Use a compass and map ruler
- Find your position and direction of travel
- Plan a realistic route
- Navigate in poor visibility
