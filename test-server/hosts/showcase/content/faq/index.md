## How fit do I need to be?

Every trip has an honest difficulty rating. A normal level of fitness is enough for trips marked Easy. Challenging treks require previous experience on multi-day hikes.

## Can I join on my own?

Absolutely. Many of our guests arrive solo. Small groups usually make it easy to settle in and connect from the first day.

## What equipment do I need?

You receive a detailed packing list well before departure. Specialist equipment such as snowshoes and group safety gear is included where stated.

## What happens in bad weather?

Our guides adapt the route and schedule. Safety comes before a fixed itinerary, while a meaningful day outside remains the goal.
