## One step at a time

This journey combines rewarding mountain paths, welcoming huts and immense views. We walk for four to six hours each day, leaving room for breaks, regional food and stories from the mountains.

## Included

- Five nights in carefully selected mountain huts
- Breakfast and dinner featuring regional food
- A qualified mountain hiking guide
- Route planning and group safety equipment
- Optional luggage transfer on selected stages
